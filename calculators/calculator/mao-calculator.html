Formula model

MAO = ARV × rule percentage − repairs − desired fee − closing costs − holding costs. Many wholesalers use the 70% rule as a starting point, but stronger or weaker markets may require adjustment.

What to check next

Verify ARV with sold comps, inspect repairs, confirm buyer appetite, and do not treat the 70% rule as universal.
