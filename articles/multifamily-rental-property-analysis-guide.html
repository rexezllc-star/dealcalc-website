How to Analyze a Duplex, Triplex, or Fourplex Investment Property

Small multifamily deals require better analysis than a single rent field. Unit count, vacancy, expense ratios, and financing all shape the deal.

Begin with gross scheduled income

Gross scheduled income is the rent you would collect if every unit were occupied and paying market rent.

Adjust for vacancy

Effective gross income subtracts vacancy and credit loss from the total income potential.

Calculate NOI

NOI equals effective income minus operating expenses before debt service. It is the foundation for cap rate and DSCR.

Review debt service coverage

DSCR compares NOI to annual debt service. Lenders often use DSCR to determine whether rental income can support the loan.
