What Is MAO in Wholesale Real Estate?

This guide is written for investors who need practical deal analysis, fast underwriting, and clear decision-making before making offers.

Overview

What Is MAO in Wholesale Real Estate? is an important part of real estate investing because it affects offer price, risk, profit margin, and exit strategy. The goal is not just to calculate a number, but to make a disciplined decision.

Investor Example

Assume a deal has a resale value of $250,000, repairs of $45,000, and target wholesale fee of $15,000. A disciplined investor would compare the purchase price against MAO and only proceed if the spread creates enough room for buyer profit.

Key Takeaways

  • Verify numbers before sending an offer.
  • Leave room for repairs, closing costs, holding costs, and buyer profit.
  • Use calculators to standardize decisions across multiple deals.
