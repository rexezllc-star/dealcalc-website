Formula model

Monthly cash flow = monthly NOI − debt service. Cash-on-cash return = annual cash flow ÷ cash invested. DSCR = NOI ÷ debt service. Break-even occupancy estimates how much income can fall before the deal stops covering expenses and debt.

Best-use guidance

Use this as a screening model before making an offer. Verify rent comps, taxes, insurance, HOA rules, lender terms, repairs, vacancy, and local landlord regulations before closing.
