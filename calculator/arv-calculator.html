Formula model

ARV = comparable sold value adjusted by subject square footage, comparable $/sq ft, condition, and location. Sold comps should carry more weight than active listings.

Best-use guidance

Use this result as a screening tool. Verify assumptions with comps, title data, inspections, lender terms, local taxes, insurance, and market conditions.
