Formula model

Total rehab budget = line-item subtotal + contingency reserve. Cost per square foot = total rehab budget ÷ property square footage. Repair risk increases when mechanicals dominate the budget, contingency is low, or cost per square foot is unusually high.

Best-use guidance

Use this as an offer-stage repair estimate. Before closing, validate with contractor walkthroughs, permit review, inspection findings, material pricing, and local labor costs.
