About

Built to help investors make faster, cleaner deal decisions.

DealCalc.io is a real estate deal analysis platform for wholesalers, land investors, flippers, and buy-and-hold investors.

The mission is simple: help investors move from rough guesses to structured analysis using calculators, educational guides, and AI-assisted deal scoring.

Who DealCalc is for

  • Wholesalers estimating MAO and assignment fees
  • Land investors checking due diligence and resale margin
  • Flippers analyzing repairs, ARV, and spread
  • Rental investors reviewing ROI, cap rate, and cash flow
