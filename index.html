Frequently asked questions

Are these calculators financial advice?

No. DealCalc provides educational estimates. Investors should verify numbers with brokers, lenders, attorneys, title companies, and tax professionals.

Why include percentages?

Percentages make deals comparable. A $5,000 profit on a $20,000 deal is very different from $5,000 on a $200,000 deal.

Can I use this for mortgage analysis?

Yes. The mortgage, DSCR, cap rate, and cash-on-cash tools support residential rental analysis.
