Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how DealCalc.io may collect and use information when visitors use the website.

Last updated: June 2026

Information we collect

DealCalc.io may collect basic website usage data such as pages viewed, device type, browser type, approximate location, referral source, and interactions with calculators or links.

Google Analytics

DealCalc.io uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to collect usage information. This helps us measure traffic, improve calculators, and decide which tools and articles to build next.

Calculator inputs

DealCalc calculators run in the browser. We do not require account registration, and we do not intentionally collect personally identifiable calculator inputs. Calculator usage events may be tracked in an aggregated way, such as when a calculator is viewed or used.

Cookies

Cookies may be used for analytics, site performance, and future functionality. Visitors can manage cookies through browser settings.

Third-party services

DealCalc may use third-party providers, including Google Analytics, Vercel, GitHub, and future advertising, affiliate, form, or email tools. These third parties may process data according to their own policies.

Contact

Questions about this policy may be sent to contact@dealcalc.io.
